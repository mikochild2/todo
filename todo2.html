Get Started

The 2:DO list is easy to use.

First, click Add New, type your task, and press Enter to add an item to the list.

To mark an item as Completed, just click it to mark through the text.

Didn't mean to add an item or need to clean up your list? Click the trash can icon to delete the item.

And that's it! Click Add New below to get started.
